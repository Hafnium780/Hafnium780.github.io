Projectile Motion With Linear Drag

\[\large{\displaylines{y''=-g-ky' \\ x''=-kx'}}\]

General solution for y:

\[y'=ae^{-kt}+b\]

Plug into differential equation to find b:

\[-kae^{-kt}=-g-k(ae^{-kt}+b)\]

\[0=-g-kb\]

\[b=-\frac{g}{k} \]

Plug in initial conditions to find a (\(y'=v\sin(\theta)\) when \(t=0\)):

\[y'=ae^{-kt}-\frac{g}{k} \]

\[v\sin(\theta)=a-\frac{g}{k} \]

\[a=v\sin(\theta)+\frac{g}{k} \]

Integrate:

\[\int{y'dt}=\int{((v\sin(\theta)+\frac{g}{k})e^{-kt}-\frac{g}{k})dt}\]

\[y=-\frac{1}{k}(v\sin(\theta)+\frac{g}{k})e^{-kt}-\frac{g}{k}t+c\]

Plug in initial conditions to find c (\(y=0\) when \(t=0\))

\[0=-\frac{1}{k}(v\sin(\theta)+\frac{g}{k})-0+c\]

\[c=\frac{1}{k}(v\sin(\theta)+\frac{g}{k})\]

Simplify:

\[y=-\frac{1}{k}(v\sin(\theta)+\frac{g}{k})e^{-kt}-\frac{g}{k}t+\frac{1}{k}(v\sin(\theta)+\frac{g}{k})\]

\[y=\frac{1}{k}(v\sin(\theta)+\frac{g}{k})(1-e^{-kt})-\frac{g}{k}t\]

\[y=\frac{(v\sin(\theta)+\frac{g}{k})(1-e^{-kt})-gt}{k}\]

General solution for x:

\[x'=ae^{-kt}+b\]

Plug into differential equation to find b:

\[-kae^{-kt}=-k(ae^{-kt}+b)\]

\[b=0\]

Plug in initial conditions to find a (\(x'=v\cos(\theta)\) when \(t=0\)):

\[v\cos(\theta)=a\]

Integrate:

\[\int{x'dt}=\int{v\cos(\theta)e^{-kt}dt}\]

\[x=-\frac{v}{k}\cos(\theta)e^{-kt}+c\]

Plug in initial conditions to find c (\(x=0\) when \(t=0\))

\[0=-\frac{v}{k}\cos(\theta)+c\]

\[c=\frac{v}{k}\cos(\theta)\]

Simplify:

\[x=-\frac{v}{k}\cos(\theta)e^{-kt}+\frac{v}{k}\cos(\theta)\]

\[x=\frac{v\cos(\theta)}{k}(1-e^{-kt})\]
